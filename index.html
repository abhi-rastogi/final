About Us

EUROCK – A Safety Revolution by Euro Safety Solutions, India!

The economical range of industrial safety footwear is an initiative to encourage companies to enhance their productivity by providing their workers the Right to Safety. Our dedicated team ensures continuous improvements in making EUROCK your most trusted name in the safety shoe range. We are focused to keep every pair of feet at the workplace, protected from all kinds of hazards. Our diversified range is designed in sync with the latest trends to give you the option to choose the style best suited to your industry. With an efficient supply chain, we aim to reach you in the fastest ways.

QUALITY & TESTING

Produced in an ISO 9001 & SA 8000 facility, every pair of EUROCK safety footwear is tested & certified by a highly equipped in-house testing lab and other international labs against EN ISO 20345 & IS 15298 norms.

Every pair undergoes rigorous quality checks at multiple stages during production to ensure that the customer receives a state-of-the-art engineered pair of the shoe –EUROCK

OUR VISION

Engineered, Affordable & Accessible range of Safety Footwear especially curated for the Indian workforce. Developed by one of the Asia’s largest Safety Equipment manufacturers in accordance to all Indian & International safety norms. EUROCK’s vision is to protect The Everyday Hero without compromising on the product, quality & testing.
